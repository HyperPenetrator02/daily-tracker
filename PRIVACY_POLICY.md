# Privacy Policy - StatMaxer RPG OS

**Last Updated:** January 19, 2026

## 100% Offline & Private

StatMaxer is committed to protecting your privacy. This application operates entirely offline on your device.

### Data Collection

**We collect ZERO data.**

- ✅ All your habit data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage
- ✅ No server communication - your data never leaves your device
- ✅ No analytics, tracking, or telemetry
- ✅ No advertisements
- ✅ No third-party services
- ✅ No account creation required

### Data Storage

All application data is stored in your browser's local storage:
- Habit configurations
- Daily completion logs
- Player profile (name, level, XP)
- Notification preferences

### Permissions

The app requests the following permissions for functionality:

1. **Notifications** - To send you habit reminders and hardcore alarms
2. **Vibrate** - For hardcore alarm vibration alerts
3. **Wake Lock** - To ensure alarms trigger even when device is sleeping

These permissions are used ONLY for local functionality. No data is transmitted.

### Data Control

You have complete control over your data:
- Export your data anytime (coming soon)
- Delete all data via Settings → Reset All Data
- Uninstall the app to remove all local data

### Open Source

StatMaxer is open source. You can review the entire codebase at:
https://github.com/HyperPenetrator02/daily-tracker

### Contact

For questions or concerns, open an issue on GitHub.
